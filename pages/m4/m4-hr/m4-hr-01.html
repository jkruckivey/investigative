The Night Shift Harassment Complaint

Your investigation begins with a formal complaint

You are an HR investigator at Maple Leaf Foods. The following complaint has been filed through the formal HR intake process. Read the details carefully—you will be working through this investigation from classification to conclusion.

Scenario

Sarah Kim, a night shift production worker at the Brampton facility, has filed a formal complaint through HR alleging that her shift supervisor, David Park, has been making inappropriate comments about her appearance for the past two months. She says two coworkers witnessed some incidents but are afraid to come forward. She reports feeling unsafe and has requested a shift transfer.

Interim measures: Before proceeding with the investigation, consider whether interim measures are needed to protect the complainant.
