Maple Leaf Foods

Enterprise-Wide Investigative Procedures

A self-paced e-learning course for investigators across HR, FSQA, and OHS

Course Overview

This course teaches the standardized investigative procedures that apply across all Maple Leaf Foods functions. Whether you investigate workplace conduct issues, food safety incidents, or occupational health and safety events, this SOP provides the common framework, tools, and quality standards for your work.

ⓘ
Estimated time: 3–4 hours (self-paced). You can leave and return at any time — your progress is saved automatically.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  1. Explain the purpose, scope, and guiding principles of the Enterprise-Wide Investigative Procedures
  2. Classify incoming complaints using the three investigation levels and the Classification & Escalation Framework
  3. Plan and conduct an investigation following best practices for evidence collection, interviews, and analysis
  4. Apply the framework to function-specific scenarios in HR, FSQA, or OHS
  5. Document findings in a clear, defensible investigation report and follow proper closure procedures

Meet the Henderson File

Anchor Case Study

Throughout this course, you will follow the Henderson File — a fictional investigation that threads through every module. You will apply each new concept to this case as you progress, building a complete investigation from intake through closure.

The Henderson File will be introduced in Module 1 and you will return to it at each stage of the investigative process.
