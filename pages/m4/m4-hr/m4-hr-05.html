Analysis & Conclusion

Weigh the evidence and reach a conclusion

The interview phase is complete. Before reaching a conclusion, review the full body of evidence gathered during this investigation.

Evidence Summary

Complainant Statement (Sarah Kim)

Sarah provided a detailed, consistent account of inappropriate comments made by David Park about her appearance on multiple occasions over a two-month period. She identified specific dates, locations within the facility, and the nature of the comments. Her account remained consistent across initial complaint and formal interview.

Witness Statements

Two coworkers independently corroborated Sarah's account. Witness 1 confirmed hearing David comment on Sarah's appearance on at least three occasions, describing specific comments that aligned with Sarah's account. Witness 2 confirmed overhearing similar comments on two occasions and noted that David's tone was "not professional." Neither witness reported being coached or prompted by Sarah.

Respondent Statement (David Park)

David denied all allegations with a blanket denial: "I have never said anything inappropriate." When presented with specific allegations including dates and details, he did not provide alternative accounts of those interactions or plausible explanations. He suggested the complaint was retaliatory because of a poor performance review he issued to Sarah. However, HR records confirm that Sarah's complaint was initiated before the performance review was delivered.

Documentary Evidence

Shift schedules confirm that Sarah and David worked the same shifts on all dates referenced. HR records show no prior complaints filed by or against either party. The performance review David referenced was dated after Sarah's initial complaint, contradicting his retaliation theory. No relevant CCTV footage was available from the production floor areas where the comments allegedly occurred.
