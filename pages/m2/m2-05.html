Severity Assessment Practice

Apply the 5-dimension severity matrix to real-world scenarios

In this activity, you will practice applying the Impact & Severity Assessment (Step 3 of the Classification Framework) to three scenarios. For each scenario, rate each of the five severity dimensions on a scale of 1 (Low) to 3 (High), then check your answers against the suggested ratings.
