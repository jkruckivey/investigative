Henderson File

You are the assigned investigator on the Henderson File. As part of your investigation into allegations that production supervisor Marcus Chen may have directed workers to skip inspection steps, you are about to interview a line worker who was present on the production floor during the period in question. The worker appears nervous as they enter the interview room.

Your choices at each stage will be evaluated based on the interview techniques covered in this module. Choose the best approach at each step.
