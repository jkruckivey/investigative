Understanding who does what during an investigation

Every investigation involves a number of distinct roles, each with specific responsibilities. Understanding these roles is essential for maintaining the integrity and fairness of the process. Click each role below to learn more about its definition and responsibilities.

ⓘ
Separation of roles: A key principle of the SOP is that the investigator and the decision-maker should be different people. This separation helps ensure objectivity and prevents any single individual from both investigating and adjudicating a matter.
