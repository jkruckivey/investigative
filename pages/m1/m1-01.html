Module 1: The Investigative Framework

Purpose & Scope

Why a standardized investigative procedure matters

Why One Enterprise-Wide Procedure?

Maple Leaf Foods operates across multiple functions, each of which may encounter situations requiring formal investigation. Previously, individual functions maintained their own investigation procedures, leading to inconsistencies in approach, documentation, and outcomes. The Enterprise-Wide Investigative Procedures SOP was created to address three critical needs:

Consistency

A single, standardized process ensures that every investigation—regardless of function or location—follows the same structured methodology. This reduces variability and ensures that similar matters are handled in a comparable manner across the organization.

Defensibility

Investigations may be subject to external scrutiny by regulators, legal counsel, or courts. A well-documented, consistently applied procedure demonstrates that the organization acted reasonably, thoroughly, and in good faith, strengthening the defensibility of outcomes and any subsequent decisions.

Compliance

Maple Leaf Foods is subject to a range of legal and regulatory requirements across its operations. A unified procedure helps ensure that investigations meet all applicable legal obligations, including employment standards, occupational health and safety regulations, food safety legislation, and privacy laws.

⚠
Important: This SOP replaces all previous function-specific investigation procedures. All investigations initiated after the SOP's effective date must follow this enterprise-wide process.
