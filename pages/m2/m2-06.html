Module 2: Intake, Classification & Escalation

Function Routing

Match investigation scenarios to the correct function

Step 5 of the Classification Framework requires routing each complaint to the function best positioned to lead the investigation. The correct routing depends on the nature of the core allegation. In this activity, you will practice matching common complaint types to the appropriate investigative function.

ⓘ
Instructions: Click a scenario on the left, then click the matching function on the right. A correct match will highlight in green. An incorrect match will flash red and allow you to try again. Continue until all pairs are matched.
