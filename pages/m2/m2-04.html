Module 2: Intake, Classification & Escalation

Escalation Triggers

Practice identifying which scenarios require immediate escalation

Not every complaint follows the standard classification path. Certain conditions require immediate escalation to Level 1 + Legal, bypassing the standard severity assessment. In this activity, you will sort scenarios into the correct category based on what you learned about escalation triggers.

ⓘ
Instructions: Drag each scenario from the "Items to Sort" column into the correct category: either "Immediate Escalation (Level 1 + Legal)" or "Standard Classification Process." Once all items are placed, you will see your results.
